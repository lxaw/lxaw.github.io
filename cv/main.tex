\section{Summary}
Machine learning researcher specializing in efficient training and inference of large language models. Currently co-lead pretraining and inference optimization research for the Sarashina LLM series at SoftBank's SB Intuitions; previously co-led NVIDIA's flagship diffusion language models (1--14B parameters), building training and inference infrastructure adopted by 5+ internal research teams. Publications at ICML, CVPR, and ACL; NSF Graduate Research Fellow. Proven record of leading research teams in both the United States and Japan.
