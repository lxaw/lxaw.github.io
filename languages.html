Language Learning

For human beings

In general, Anki will be your best bet. The idea is to memorize using Anki, and learn via immersion, specifically using the input hypothesis. I did this immersion method for about ~3 years with Japanese and was able to be fluent enough to go to college with regular students in Tokyo. It works, just trust the method.
